About Us

Who we are ?

We are a global talent acquisition firm that serves clients across all major industry segments and domains. Our focus is to help organizations find world-class talent and our success is rooted in our commitment to understand your goals, values and people strategies.We provide advice and solutions to our clients that are product, platform and technology independent. Whether it involves providing onsite-consulting services or developing customized software solutions on-site or offshore, Tekhyre Consulting continuously transforms its expertise into productivity gains for its clients”



Culture and Values ?

We established a set of values that drives results, long-term partnerships and value.

  • Partnership– We work as an extension of your internal recruitment
  • Integrity– We operate with an unparalleled level of honesty and ethics in everything that we do.
  • Transparency– All that we do is open to review.
